Who is Norsmither?

Hello, thank you for reading this. I'm Khanh, also a Norsmither, I created this website to introduce myself, to let you guys know who I really am, and what I'm doing.
Basically, I'm just an ordinary person with a simple mindset in a complicated world and a burning passion, which is music.
Welcome to my world, my friend.

Why "Norsmither"?

In fact, the original meaning of the name "Norsmither" was "Normal Smith". "Normal" implies that I'm just an ordinary person, nothing special, and "Smith" is the name of someone I admire a lot named Jaden Smith. Thus, the name "Norsmither" was born.
I love this name very much and it came to me as an arrangement. My friend told me to find myself a meaningful and special name. Now that I have a name that cannot be more meaningful and special, for me this name is already great, I feel confident.

My Job/IT

Yes, that's right, my job is programming. Honestly, I don't really like this job even though I'll be graduating next year since I'm writing this (haha).
Because I don't really like this job, it means learning to code is very difficult for me. I have poured a lot of money into learning just to acquire knowledge. After all, I don't want to give up again. Now I like this challenge. I pursue programming not because of passion, but because of challenge.

My passion/Music

I have to admit that I really love music even though it's not my day job. Surely someone will ask why I don't choose music as my daily job? Actually, there are many answers to that question, but from what I'm going through, the only answer is: I don't want to be confined to a daily job that I have to do. I want freedom - freedom of imagination - freedom of time - freedom the way I want.
From here people will be able to recognize two differences in themselves. If Tan Khanh is someone for his day job of programming, Norsmither is someone for music, and I like that because I can live two different personalities.
