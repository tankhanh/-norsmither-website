INTRODUCE

Norsmither

Hello everyone, I'm Norsmither. This website is to introduce myself as well as to introduce music-related activities that I will launch in the near future.
Before talking about the reason for creating this website, I would like to introduce a little about myself, my real name is Dinh Tan Khanh, born in 2000, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. I graduated from Van Lang University, majoring in Software Engineering. In addition to being a programming engineer, I also have a passion for music and especially being able to sing, compose and produce music. Through my work as a software developer, I want to show people what I can do with music as well as program software. I thought it was a good idea because I was able to combine my two jobs (programming and music) and now it's becoming a reality.
Since this is the first time developing the project, everything will be constantly updated to make my website more and more perfect. Hope this site will be liked by everyone and give me lots of constructive feedback!
In conclusion, thank you all very much for visiting my website. If you have any cool ideas about music or website development, don't hesitate to contact me via inbox (Below). Thank you very much!
